SKEDULE APP

PROJECT DESCRIPTION

The purpose of this project is to attempt to strengthen the social fabric on university campuses by enabling college students to easily coordinate activities like studying or leisure with their friends. The goal is to design an application that asks the user to indicate when they plan on studying and when they plan on enjoying free time throughout the week and shows the user how their plans compares with their friends.

DESIGN PROBLEM

Planning study sessions or leisure time with friends should be easy, but it’s not. In college, everyone is on different schedules between courses, extracurriculars, and other commitments. It can take several different messages between students just to find a time that works. I think this back-and-forth can be eliminated with an app concept that I’m calling Skedule. In the app, users would create a schedule of when they plan on studying or enjoying free time throughout the week, observe how their schedule compared to their friends, and make adjustments when desirable. The platform would also facilitate messaging between friends whose schedules overlapped, so that they could meetup and study or have fun together.
